Travnik- Vezirski grad

Nestled in the heart of central Bosnia and Herzegovina, Travnik is a town steeped in history and adorned with captivating Ottoman architecture. This picturesque destination served as the capital of the Ottoman province of Bosnia in the 15th and 16th centuries, leaving behind a legacy of cultural richness. Visitors are drawn to landmarks like the iconic Travnik Castle, the vibrant Old Bazaar, and the elegant Sulejmanija Mosque. Beyond its historical charm, Travnik invites exploration of the surrounding natural beauty, offering a delightful blend of cultural heritage and scenic landscapes.
